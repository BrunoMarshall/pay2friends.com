About Pay2Friends

Pay2Friends is a decentralized payment platform built on the Shardeum Unstablenet, designed to make crypto payments as simple as sending an email. Our mission is to empower anyone to send SHM, ERC-20 or our native P2F tokens to friends using their wallet address or email, all without intermediaries. Launched in 2025, Pay2Friends leverages blockchain technology to ensure secure, transparent, and community-driven transactions.

Our Vision

We aim to redefine peer-to-peer payments by combining the ease of traditional apps like PayPal with the power of decentralization. Our goal is to build a platform that’s accessible to everyone, from crypto beginners to seasoned users. We’re inspired by the legendary PayPal Mafia, a group of innovators who transformed fintech, and we’re seeking passionate developers, designers, and visionaries to join our team and make Pay2Friends the next big thing in crypto.

The P2F Token

The P2F token is our native token on Pay2Friends:

  • Contract Address: 0x30d16e2bd13bfdf42a47cc18468240bc3bed69ff
  • Network: Shardeum Unstablenet (Chain ID: 8080)
  • Type: ERC-20
  • Purpose: Used for peer-to-peer payments within Pay2Friends, enabling fast and low-cost transactions.
  • Features: Fully integrated with our smart contract for email-based transfers and transaction logging.

The P2F token is designed to incentivize community participation and could evolve to support governance or rewards as the platform grows.

Future Ideas

We have big plans for Pay2Friends, and we’re excited to brainstorm with our future team. Some ideas include:

  • Mobile Apps: Develop iOS and Android apps to bring Pay2Friends to mobile users, making crypto payments as easy as a tap.
  • Email Verification: Explore solutions like Firebase Authentication for email ownership verification, while discussing fully decentralized alternatives (e.g., Sign-in with Ethereum or Ceramic) to maintain our ethos.
  • Transaction History: Add a feature to display past transfers, pulling data from the blockchain’s TransferLogged events.
  • Social Features: Enhance the friends list with social discovery, allowing users to find and connect with others on the platform.
  • Multi-Chain Support: Expand to other blockchains for broader adoption.

These ideas will be refined with input from our community and team to ensure they align with our decentralized vision.

Preventing Email Spoofing

Currently, users can register any email with our smart contract, which poses a risk of spoofing. To address this, we propose introducing a small registration fee (e.g., 1 P2F or 100 SHM) to deter fake registrations. This fee could be:

  • Refundable after a certain period or burned to support the ecosystem.
  • Used to fund community initiatives or platform development.
  • Adjustable based on network activity to balance accessibility and security.

This proposal needs team discussion to ensure it’s fair and aligns with our goal of accessibility. We’re also exploring decentralized identity solutions to verify email ownership without centralized services.
