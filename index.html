Simple Crypto Payments for Everyone

Pay2Friends is a decentralized peer-to-peer payment system built on Shardeum.

Send, receive, and track crypto payments easily for games, local trade, and social sharing, like Venmo/PayPal but powered by blockchain on steroids.

Get Started

  1. Connect your MetaMask wallet.
  2. Register your email address to your wallet.
  3. Send money or payments via email or account address.
